### 🛠️ Architectural Highlights

- **Data-Driven Execution**: Every module can pull target data (SSID/BSSID/CH) from the initial `A1` discovery results, ensuring zero-latency pivoting during an engagement.
- **Unified Safety Engine**: Global trap handling and PID tracking prevent zombie processes from disrupting client infrastructure during aborts.
- **Dynamic Customization**: Attacks involving user interaction (Evil Twin, Captive Portals) are fully configurable with custom SSIDs and HTML templates.
- **Structured Evidence Chain**: PCAPs, logs, and tool outputs are organized into session-specific directories, with results aggregated into a finalized HTML report.
